Director's Message

Dear Friends

Yadavindra College of Engineering is on a fascinating path of growth and development. As you are aware, it has already established itself as one of the top technological institutes in the state, but that is just the tip of the iceberg compared to the huge initiatives and progress the institute is making. The instituteâ€™s own campus on 85 acres pristine land, on the land of Dashmesh Pitta Sri Guru Gobind Singh ji's Sri Damdama Sahib. The institute has increased its student strength from a meager 650 in 2015 to over 1100 today which is likely to be increased to 2500 by 2021.

Our faculty strength has increased during the last two years from 65 to 115. We are expecting this to increase to 250 by 2019. Apart from existing departments of Computer Science, Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering, we have recently started the Certificate Courses. We have expanded our Post Graduate Programmes (M.Tech) to the domains of Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Engineering. Yadavindra College of Engineering has got a very strong basic science component with MSc. degrees being taught in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics department. PhD programme is one of the major strength of Yadavindra College with approximately 5% of the student strength being PhD research scholars. The research is a major focus of Yadavindra College which is evident from the fact that we have got the highest scientific citations per paper among all the new Engineering Colleges, which is an indicator of high quality of research done here.

The excellence in teaching and research comes from the careful choice of faculty members in which Yadavindra College of Engineering has not made any compromise. It has gone to outreach and fetch scholars from all over the world to enrich its faculty strength and quality. This year, we have introduced the newly designed curriculum which is based on the principle of more hands on experience with strong fundamentals offering more choices to the students and imbibing social and national commitments in them. Yadavindra College of Engineering has expanded its outreach to industry and the best academic institution in the world by entering into active collaborations with them. Research collaboration is not an additional feather in our cap but an integral part of our growth strategy.

Recently Yadavindra College of Engineering has adopted a new mission and vision for the institute according to which we want to become a leader among the institutes established in the new millennium. We have made a stunning beginning but there is a long way to go but I am sure, with the active cooperation of all our stakeholders, students, faculty, staff, employees, collaborators, parents of the students, governmental organizations, media and public at large, we will be able to fulfill the motto of Yadavindra College of Engineering,

Vision & Mission

Yadavindra College of Engineering community has undertaken an important exercise of developing the Mission, Vision and Strategic Plan for the coming years. The exercise was carried out in active consultation with Punjabi University, Patiala who have got technical expertise in this area. A participative bottom up approach was followed in formulating the Mission, Vision and Strategic Plan. Inputs from the entire Yadavindra College fraternity, MHRD, Board of Governors, Alumni and Industries were considered in the process. Finally we have arrived at the following Motto, Mission and Vision statements for our institute.

Motto: (True learning induces in the mind, service to mankind.)

Mission: To foster a transformative learning environment and a culture of excellence enabling creation of knowledge and development of socially responsible, enterprising leaders contributing significantly to national progress and humanity.

Vision: To be a trendsetter among the technology universities born in this millennium.
